Where We Came From

We Built Factor42 Because We Felt Every Pain Our Customers Feel Today.

"We watched talented media teams drowning in trafficking queues, missed launches, and fragmented dashboards — while the real work of strategy and relationships sat on the back burner. Something had to change."

— The Factor42 Founding Team

After 20+ combined years working across agencies, publishers, DSPs, and media companies — we saw the same story play out everywhere: brilliant digital teams spending 70% of their time on execution, 30% on strategy, when it should be the reverse. The platforms kept multiplying. The audience segment demands kept growing. The headcount budgets stayed flat.

The Problem

Teams crushed by operational complexity

Platform proliferation meant more DSPs, more ad servers, more reporting sources — and the same number of people expected to handle it all. Trafficking backlogs. Missed flights. Budget waste.

The Realization

Headcount isn't the answer — expertise and systems are

Hiring more junior traffickers created more management overhead, not more capacity. What teams needed wasn't more bodies — they needed a better-architected system run by people who'd done this thousands of times.

The Answer

Factor42 — expert humans, amplified by AI

We built the team we always wished we could hire: deeply experienced ad ops specialists, backed by AI agents that eliminate the repetitive work so humans can focus purely on outcomes.

Our Mission

Freeing Digital Teams to Do What Only Humans Can.

We exist to remove the operational burden from digital teams so they can focus on strategy, relationships, and decisions that move the needle — not trafficking queues and dashboard wrangling.

Expert People, Always

Every client engagement is owned by a seasoned ad ops professional who has worked across the platforms, seen the edge cases, and knows what good looks like. No juniors learning on your campaigns.

AI That Actually Works

AI agents run through every layer of our operations — from automated QA to intelligent pacing alerts to report generation. Not a pilot. Not a demo. Live in production, scaling our capacity every single day.

Humans at Every Decision

AI surfaces, synthesizes, and accelerates — but every judgment call that affects your campaigns, your budget, and your customers is made by an experienced human. That's non-negotiable at Factor42.

Our Technology Philosophy

AI Is Built Into Everything. Humans Run the Show.

We don't bolt AI onto an existing workflow as an afterthought. AI agents are woven into the fabric of how Factor42 operates — handling the high-volume, rules-based work that previously consumed expert hours.

This means our people spend their time where experience actually matters: diagnosing pacing issues, making optimization calls, managing client relationships, and exercising the kind of judgment that no model can replicate.

The result is a service that scales at a rate previously impossible — not by cutting corners on quality, but by eliminating everything that shouldn't require human attention in the first place.

AI handles

Creative QA, spec compliance checks, data aggregation, pacing alerts, report generation, anomaly detection, and trafficking task routing.

Our people decide

Every optimization strategy, every client escalation, every campaign launch approval, and every judgment call on what's best for the customer's outcome.

The outcome

Scale that wasn't previously possible — without sacrificing the quality, accountability, and expertise your campaigns depend on.
